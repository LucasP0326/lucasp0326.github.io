Red Horizon

On a partially-terraformed Mars, the powers of Earth compete for planetary dominance, rekindling the sparks of total warfare in what should be an age of peace and exploration. Players, the sole-survivors of the destruction of "The Creator," a research and terraforming station, now find themselves at the center of a mystery that may very well determine the future of an entire planet.

A hypothetical game meant to demonstrate capacity for narrative design and consistent direction.
