Creative Direction

As Creative Director, guiding the creative flow of the game was my responsibility. I was responsible for the creation of our GDD, ensuring art consistency, narrative continuity, etc..

Level Design

Designed our game's levels alongside to ensure visual style, storytelling consistency, and adherence to project objectives as well as worked in engine utilizing art assets, scripts, lighting, etc. to handcraft these levels.

Narrative Design

Worked as chief Narrative Designer, crafting our story from the ground up, writing dialogue for encounters, cutscenes, simple text bites, etc., as well as overseeing voice recording to ensure consistency and quality..

Code

Worked directly on our code team, designing and scripting encounters, coding in numerous features such as health, enemy AI, special abilities, etc., and more.
